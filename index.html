Sri Sangaveda Pathasala Shankara Matham

Sangaveda Pathasala was established in 1928, near sri Nageswara swamy Devasthanam to restore the importance of vedas. In the year of 1939, it was relocated to a place donated by Late Kasturi Krishanandam in Godugupet and with others' cooperation Shankara Matham was founded and Sri Gayathri, Shankara, Dattatreya were enshrined. Since then, it became a centre of spritual activities. Later in 1975, it was taken control by Sri Kanchi Kamakoti Peetham and had gotten more progress.
